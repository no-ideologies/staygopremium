About Stay Go Premium

Since 2014, Stay Go Premium has been the UK's leading luxury RV rental company, serving motorsport, festival, and equestrian events across the UK and Europe.

Based in the beautiful Cotswolds, we deliver premium American RVs and fifth wheels to your event, handle all setup, and provide 24/7 support throughout your stay.

From Le Mans to Glastonbury, from the British Grand Prix to Badminton Horse Trials, we've provided luxury accommodation at hundreds of events, earning a reputation for exceptional service and premium quality.
