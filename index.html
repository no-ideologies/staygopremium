About StayGo Premium

StayGo Premium was founded to bring a new level of luxury and convenience to event accommodation across the UK. We specialize in delivering premium American fifth wheels and RVs to major motorsport events, music festivals, equestrian gatherings, and TV/film productions.

Unlike larger operators, we focus on providing personalized, boutique service. Every client receives our full attention, and every detail is taken care of. From the moment you enquire to the moment we collect your accommodation, we're here to ensure your experience is flawless.

Based in the heart of the Cotswolds, we've built strong relationships with event organizers across the UK and know the best locations at each venue. When you book with StayGo Premium, you're not just getting accommodation—you're getting years of expertise and a commitment to excellence.

  • Quality First: Premium vehicles and service
  • Personal Touch: Boutique service, not a number
  • Event Expertise: Years of experience at major UK events
